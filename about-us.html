Payment Methods

Why Timeza Uses EFT Payments

At Timeza, we prioritize security, transparency, and value for our customers. That's why we use EFT (Electronic Funds Transfer) as our primary payment method. EFT payments allow us to process orders securely and efficiently, ensuring every transaction is verified before your order is dispatched.

By using EFT, we're able to keep our prices competitive while maintaining the high level of trust and reliability our customers expect from Timeza.

Payment Methods

Benefits of EFT Payments

Enhanced Security - EFT transactions take place directly between your bank and ours, reducing the risk of third-party fraud.

Better Value for You - By avoiding high credit card processing fees, we can pass those savings on to you through better pricing and exclusive offers.
